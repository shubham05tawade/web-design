Even & Odd Integers

If an integer is divisible by 2, it is called as an even number. It is represented as 2n

If an integer is not divisible by 2, it is called as an odd number. It is represented as 2n+1

Addition & Subtraction:

E + E = E

E + O = O

O + O = E

Multiplication:

E X E = E

E X O = E

O X O = O

0 is considered as even integer.

Prime Numbers

If an integer has two distinct factor which is one and itself, it is called as prime numbers.

0, 1 and negative numbers are not prime numbers

2 is the smallest prime number.

2 is the only even prime number. All other prime numbers are odd.

List of prime no. from 1 to 100:

GCD & LCM

GCD stands for greatest common factor and LCM stands for least common multiple.

Consider a=60, b=210

Factors of 60 are: 2 X 2 X 3 X 5

Factors of 210 are: 2 X 3 X 5 X 7

Pick the factor which is found common in both of them and multiply. Here GCD of (60, 210) is 2 X 3 X 5 = 30

LCM is prouct of GCM and the remaining factors which aren't common in both. Here LCM of (60, 120) is GCD X 2 X 7 = 420

Formula:

a x b = GCD x LCM

Ratio and Proprtions

In a class, student who has selected English, French and German are in ratio 3 : 2 : 4. Find the number of students learning in French if total number of students in class are 81?

Ans: So the number of students who has selected French is 2 / (3 + 2 + 4) = 2 / 9 of the total number of students in class.

Therefore, the number of students who has selected French is (2 / 9) x 81 = 18

Percentage

Percent sttands for the number divided by 100. e.g. what is 68%? ans: 68/100

e.g. Find x % y ? x/100 * y

Key points to remember:

In percentage word problem 'is' stands for = and 'of' stands for multiplication.

e.g. 6 is what percent of 25? ans: 6 = a x 25/100

Percentage Change: The Change / Original Value

e.g. what is percentage increase from 50 to 10? (50 - 10)/50 x 100 = 80

Formula:

Find 19% of 60?

We know that 10% of 60 = 6. So 20% of 60 would be 6 x 2 = 12

1% of 60 = 0.6

So to obtain 19% of 60 subtract the two result 12 - 0.6 = 11.4
