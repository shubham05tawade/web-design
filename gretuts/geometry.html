    Types of Triangles
  • Equilateral Triangle: Sides are equal in length. Angles are also equal and measures 60o
  • Right-angled Triangle: One of the angle of triangle equal measures 90o. There are two types of right angled triangle. 45-45-90 and 30-60-90.

Co-ordinate Geometry

Distance Formula: √((x1 - x2)2 + (y1 - y2)2)

Mid Point Formula: [((x1 - x2) / 2) , ((y1 - y2) / 2)]

Slope-Intercept Form: y = mx + c where m is a slope of line and c is a y-intercept.

Point-Slope Form: (y - y1) = m(x - x1) where m is a slope of line and (x1, y1) is the point through which line passes.

Two-Point Form: (y - y1) / (y2 - y1) = (x - x1) / (x2 - x1) where (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) are two points through which line is passing.

Double-Intercept Form: (x / a) + (y / b) = 1 where a is a x-intercept and b is a y-intercept.

If two lines whose slope are m1 and m2 are parallel to each other then m1 = m2

If two lines whose slope are m1 and m2 are perpendicular to each other then m1 x m2 = -1
